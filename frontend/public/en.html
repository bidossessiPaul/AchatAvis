Practical guide

How to get more Google reviews: the 9 methods

Ask for the review at the right moment, on the right channel, with the shortest possible path — here's how to apply it in practice.

1

Get your review link

In your Google Business Profile, click "Ask for reviews" to get your direct link (g.page/r/…). It opens straight to the 5-star form.

2

Ask while it's fresh

Request the review right after a positive experience: end of service, successful delivery, payment received. Positive emotion drives up the completion rate.

3

Send the link by SMS

Open rates near 98%. A short message with the direct link, sent within the hour, remains the best-performing channel for tradespeople.

4

Follow up by email

For e-commerce and B2B, a personalized email with a single "Leave a review" button a few days after purchase works well.

5

Display a QR code

In-store or in a waiting room, a QR code on the counter or receipt captures the review during downtime. Generate a free QR code.

6

Add the link to your signature

Email signature, invoice, quote, order confirmation: every touchpoint becomes a chance to collect a review with no extra effort.

7

Train your team

Frontline staff can ask for the review out loud. Set a simple goal and track it every week.

8

Reply to every review

Thanking positive reviews and calmly handling negative ones shows you're active — a signal that encourages other customers to post too.

9

Make it a habit

Listings that win build review requests into their daily process: a steady flow rather than a one-off spike.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How can I get more Google reviews with AchatAvis?

Create your account, provide your listing and the number of reviews you want. Real local guides near your area write authentic reviews, published gradually to stay natural. You track every review from your dashboard.

Are the reviews authentic and risk-free?

Yes. Our reviews are written by real Google Local Guides, never by bots. Publishing is gradual and varied to stay credible, which protects your listing from removals and suspensions.

How many Google reviews do you need to be credible?

A minimum of 10 reviews is reassuring, but local credibility really kicks in beyond 40 to 50 reviews, with an average rating above 4.3 and recent reviews published regularly. Volume matters, but freshness matters even more.

How can I earn money as a local guide?

Sign up as a guide, pick available missions around you, publish an authentic review and submit proof. Once validated, the amount is credited to your balance and paid out according to your payment history.

Is it legal to ask customers for reviews?

Yes, asking a genuine customer for a review is allowed by Google. What's forbidden is offering something in exchange for a positive review, or only asking your most satisfied customers (review gating).

How long does it take for the first reviews to appear?

The first reviews usually appear within days of launching your campaign. Publishing then stays deliberately gradual and spread over several weeks to remain natural in Google's eyes.

What happens if Google removes a review?

This stays rare thanks to gradual publishing and verified local guides, but if a review were to be removed, it is replaced as part of your order. Your dashboard keeps a full record of every review published.

Do you operate outside of France?

Yes. Beyond mainland France, we support businesses in Belgium, French-speaking Switzerland and French-speaking Africa (Benin, Senegal, Ivory Coast), with the same standard of authenticity for every review published.
